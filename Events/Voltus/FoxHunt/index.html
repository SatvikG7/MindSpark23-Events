# Event

The objective of this contest is to locate a hidden radio transmitter using radio direction-finding techniques. Participants should build the antenna and use equipment to track down the source of a transmitted signal that serves as the 'fox'. The hidden transmitter, or 'fox,' emits radio signals intermittently or continuously on a specific frequency or set of frequencies, and the participants must use their skills and equipment to determine the direction from which the signal is coming.

# Rules

  1. Participants will be required to bring their college ID cards.
  2. The first round of the contest will be an online MCQ quiz based on wireless communication and general knowledge related to the communication domain.
  3. If a team is caught cheating or playing by unfair means, then the team will be disqualified.
  4. Top 5 teams from Round 1 will be qualified for the Final on-Campus round.
  5. The decision of the respective Quiz Head will be final and will not be subject to any change.
  6. In Round 1, the teams will be judged based on accuracy and time of response.
  7. Replacement of any participant is strictly not allowed.
  8. If the no. of participating teams falls below 5, Round 2 will be conducted directly.
